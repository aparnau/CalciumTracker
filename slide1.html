Why is calcium important for your health?

Calcium is an important mineral which:

  • Helps maintain strong bones
  • Helps in muscle movement
  • Helps carry nerve messages between the brain and body parts
  • Helps in the blood circulation
  • Helps in the release of hormones and enzymes

Source: NIH. Calcium: Fact sheet for consumers. Available at: http://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Calcium-QuickFacts/#h6
