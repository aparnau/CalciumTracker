What are the foods that provide calcium?

  • Cereals and pulses such as ragi, bengal gram (whole), horse gram (whole), rajmah and soya bean
  • Green leafy vegetables such as amaranth, cauliflower greens, curry leaves, agathi and colocasia leaves
  • Nuts and oilseeds such as coconut dry, almond, mustard seeds and sunflower seeds, gingelly seeds, cumin seeds
  • Seafoods such as bacha, katla, mrigal,rohu and prawns
  • Milk, curd, paneer, cheese, skimmed milk powder and whole milk powder

Source: NIN. Dietary guidelines for Indians: A manual. National Institute of Nutrition, Hyderabad. 2010. Available at: http://ninindia.org/DietaryguidelinesforIndians-Finaldraft.pdf
